Phantom Thrust (LV1)
Souped-up version of Candy Trust.
Underground Express (LV1)
Souped-up version of Going Underground. Instead of bursting out of the ground to hit the enemy, Vegetto will instead fire an energy beam out through the ground, doing multiple hits.
8-Ball Candy (LV1)
Souped-up version of Candy Pool. If the enemy is hit by either of the first two aura-dashes, they'll be juggled around by another volley of multiple multi-directional attacks. As a final note, all three of his Level 1 Supers can be chained into each other with some care and skill.
Final Kamehameha (LV3)
Vegetto is perfectly capable of unleashing this attack even in candy form!
